Controls:
Esc - Exit game

M - Enable multisampling
N - Disable multisampling

1 - Switch to orthogonal camera
2 - Switch to fixed perspective camera
3 - Switch to a perspective camera that follows the car

W - Accelerate
S - Accelerate back
A - Turn left
D - Turn right

O - Toggle directional light
P - Toggle point lights
H - Toggle headlights
F - Toggle fog
T - Toggle debug text

Left mouse click + mouse movement - Rotate camera
Right moouse click + mouse movement - Zoom in/out
Mouse wheel - Zoom in/out
